Start a new build
The PC Assembler builder allows you to select components to put into a new build and test for compatibility and price. You can also save a build and return to it later.


The builder is very easy to use:


- Look through the list of components below and select each one by one by clicking anywhere inside the white box for that component.

- Once you reach the end and have selected all of the parts you wish to use, you can press save to save the build to your account, or you can press finish to work out total price and compatibility.

- Don't worry, you can always save your build even after selecting finish.
